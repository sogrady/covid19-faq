---
title: 'Where has it spread?'
description: 'This describes the spread of COVID-19'
---

The virus is worldwide. It has most heavily impacted China, and then South Korea and Italy, but it is in dozens of countries, on every continent except Antarctica, and is continuing to spread. As of this writing (3/2), cases within the United States seem to be accelerating. 

A site maintained by Johns Hopkins CSSE [here](https://gisanddata.maps.arcgis.com/apps/opsdashboard/index.html#/bda7594740fd40299423467b48e9ecf6) has the most up to date information the spread of the pathogen.
