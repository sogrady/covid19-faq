---
title: 'Glossary'
description: 'This is a glossary of terms used in describing the outbreak'
---
* **CFR**: Case Fatality Rate, the proportion of cases who die
* **Contact Tracing**: The process of backtracing the individuals an infected person may have had contact with
* **IFR**: Infection Fatality Rate, the proportion of all of those infected who die
* **Infectious Period**: The period of time during which an infected person may transmit the virus to others
* **R0**: Pronounced "R naught," an estimate of the number of individuals an infected person will transmit an infection to
* **Serial Interval**: The average time it takes for an infected person to transmit the infection to others
